Doug Goodwin is a professor at CalArts teaching CSCM 373-01 "Fuzzbox Physics" — a course tracing sound from air vibration through transduction, amplification, and distortion. He builds interactive browser-based audio labs as the primary teaching tools. He values hands-on, visual explanations of audio concepts and designs worksheets with step-by-step exercises that connect what students see (waveforms, spectra, spectrograms) to what they hear.
